Objective:

This project was conducted to explore sales performance trends at McDonald’s, identify product-level insights, and recommend data-backed strategies for revenue growth.

What I Did:
I built an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze product performance, time-based sales patterns, and monthly/category-level revenue distribution.

Key Findings:
1. Meatball Marinara generated the highest revenue, while Side Salad had the most orders but lower value: potential bundling strategy

2. Mondays showed the strongest sales, with dips midweek: opportunity for targeted promotions

3. Peak order times were 12-2 p.m. and 5-7 p.m.: helpful for staffing and marketing alignment

4. Underperforming items (e.g., wraps, shakes): can be repositioned via combos or limited offers

5. January outperformed Feb-Mar: suggests the need for seasonal campaign planning


Why It Matters:
This dashboard functions as a business decision-support tool, connecting sales data to actionable insights for product strategy, operations, and marketing.
